Welcome to Green INFINITY Sierra Leone

Green INFINITY Sierra Leone project aims to promte conservation through TREE PLANTING,RECYCLING AND COMMUNITY EDUCATION

This initiative will not only protect natural resources but also create jobs,generate income and support sustainable development aligned with the country's medium term national development plan and the UN sustainable development goals.[SDG's 13,15 and 11].

Executive Summary

Sierra Leone faces grozing environmental challages such as deforestation,wildlife loss,poor waste management and land degradation. The GREEN INFITNIY SIERRA LEONE PROJECT aims to promote conservation through tree planting, eco-tourism,recycling and community education.

Problem Statememt

Sierra Leone's forestsmildlife and water resources are under threat from deforestation illegal mining, bush burning and urban waste pollution many communities depend on natural resources for survival, often exploiting them unsustainably. To address this there is a need for a business oriented conservation model that combines environmental protection zith community income generation.

Wild Life Loss

Wild life in Sierra Leone faces serious thrat due to habitat destruction,poaching and deforestartion.Species such as chimpanzees and pygym hippos are declining rapidly.

Land Dagradation

Land degradation in Sierra Leone is mainly caused by unsustainable farming practice,deforestation,mining and overgrazing.continous use of the land without proper soil managment has led to soil erossion,nutrient loss and reduce agricultural productivity.

Deforestation

Deforestation is a major environmental problem in Sierra Leone,driven by charcoal production.logging.agricultural expansion and urban development.Large areas have been cleared especially in the western urban,northern and eastern provices.

Poor Waste Managment

Poor waste managment especially in urban areas like freetown has become a grozing concern.Inadequate waste collection sysytems,open dumping and burning has led to waste pollution,disease outbrake and blocked drainage systems.
